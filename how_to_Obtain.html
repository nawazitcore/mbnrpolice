How to Obtain

Investigation Documents ▼

WHAT ARE THE DOCUMENTS AVAILABLE

  1. First Information Report
  2. Inquest Report
  3. Panchanama of scene
  4. Scene of offence sketch
  5. Postmortem Examination Report
  6. Medical certificate
  7. Inspector of Factories Report
  8. Forensic Science Lab Report
  9. Chemical Analysis Report
  10. Registration Certificate of crime vehicle
  11. Driving licence of accused driver
  12. Fitness certificate of crime vehicle
  13. Insurance of crime vehicle
  14. Charge Sheet
  15. Final report

PROCEDURE

  1. Obtain application from nearest eSeva centre
  2. Pay Rs.100/- in eSeva towards user charges and Rs.60/= service charges
  3. Fill the application furnish :

FIR No And Date

  1. Relation with the victim / deceased Name and phone no of counsel
  2. Submit to eSeva Centre
  3. Approach nearest police station
  4. Obtain the certificate
  5. Will be delivered within 21 days

What are missing / Lost Documents ▼

WHAT ARE MISSING / LOST DOCUMENTS

    1. Transfer Certificate
    2. Memo of marks
    3. Driving Licence
    4. Insurance certificate
    5. Share Certificate
    6. Identity cards
    7. Vehicle Registration Certificate
    8. Vehicle Insurance
    9. Vehicle Fitness
    10. Bond Certificate
    11. Arm Licence
    12. Credit / Debit Cards
    13. Cell Phone
    14. Passport
    15. Immoveable property documents

PROCEDURE

  1. Obtain application from nearest eSeva centre
  2. Pay Rs.100/- in eSeva towards user charges and Rs.60/= service charges
  3. Pay Rs.500/- in e Seva towards user charges for Passport missing and Rs.60/= towards services
  4. Fill the application
  5. Clearly mention date, time and place of loss / missing
  6. Submit to eSeva Centre
  7. Will be delivered within 21 day

BUNDOBUST & PERMISSIONS

Marriage Function-issued by SDPO Office ▼

MARRIAGE FUNCTION-ISSUED BY SDPO OFFICE

    1. Marriage function requiring police bundobust
    2. Orchestra for small programs

PROCEDURE

  1. Obtain application from nearest eSeva centre
  2. Pay requisite fee in eSeva
  3. Fill the application
  4. Clearly mention capacity of venue
  5. Number of VIPs likely to visit
  6. Names of VIPs and category of security available
  7. Date and time of function
  8. Route of VIP
  9. Enclose wedding card / invitation card
  10. Venue permission letter
  11. Submit the application in the eSeva
  12. Permission will be granted within 7 days

Other Functions - Issued by SP Office ▼

OTHER FUNCTIONS - ISSUED BY SP OFFICE

    1. Commercial displays
    2. Corporate Anniversary function
    3. Cinema success function
    4. Industrial exhibitions
    5. Customer Meet
    6. Rallies/li>
    7. Religious functions
    8. Public Meetings

PROCEDURE

  1. Obtain application from nearest eSeva centre
  2. Pay requisite fee in eSeva
  3. Fill the application
  4. Clearly mention capacity of venue
  5. Number of VIPs likely to visit
  6. Names of VIPs and category of security available
  7. Date and time of function
  8. Route of VIP
  9. Route (in case of rallies, religious functions and public meetings)
  10. Enclose wedding card / invitation card
  11. Venue permission letter
  12. Submit the application in the eSeva
  13. Permission will be granted within 7 days

Cultural / Commercial Activities - Issued by SP Office ▼

CULTURAL / COMMERCIAL ACTIVITIES - ISSUED BY SP OFFICE

    1. Commercial
    2. Cultural show/fashion show / personality contest (commercial)
    3. Cultural show / fashion show / personality contest (non commercial)

PROCEDURE

  1. Obtain application from nearest eSeva centre
  2. Pay requisite fee in eSeva
  3. Rs.2000/- application and Rs.500/- per day for commercial
  4. Rs. 200/- application and Rs.500/- per day for non commercial
  5. Fill the application
  6. Location
  7. Purpose of show
  8. Date and duration of show
  9. Venue permission letter
  10. Submit the application in e-Seva
  11. Permission will be delivered within 7 day

Loud Speakers - Issued by SDPO ▼

LOUD SPEAKERS - ISSUED BY SDPO


PROCEDURE

  1. Obtain application from nearest eSeva centre
  2. Pay requisite fee Rs.100/- per day in e Seva
  3. Fill the application
  4. Clearly mention the purpose for which application made
  5. No of days permission required
  6. Date from which to till date permission required
  7. No of box type speakers to be installed
  8. Venue permission letter
  9. Invitation card if any
  10. Submit the application in e-Seva
  11. Permission will be granted within 7 days by concerned DSP

Mobile Advertisement for Autos / Vans - Issued by SDPO ▼

MOBILE ADVERTISEMENT FOR AUTOS / VANS - ISSUED BY SDPO


PROCEDURE

  1. Obtain application from nearest eSeva centre
  2. Pay requisite fee of Rs.500/- per day for autos and Rs.1000/- per day for vans in e Seva
  3. Fill the application
  4. Clearly mention the type of vehicle
  5. Details of the products to be advertized
  6. Location and route
  7. Date and duration permission required
  8. Brochure if any
  9. Submit the application in the eSeva
  10. Permission will be granted within 7 days by SDP

Benefits Shows - Issued by SP Office ▼

BENEFIT SHOWS - ISSUED BY SP OFFICE


PROCEDURE

  1. Obtain application from nearest eSeva centre
  2. Pay fee of Rs.1000/- per day in e Seva
  3. Fill the application
  4. Clearly mention the type of theatre
  5. Location
  6. Name of the film
  7. Date and duration
  8. Letter from Charitable trust
  9. Purpose of benefit show
  10. Submit the application in the eSeva

Certificates ▼

CERTIFICATES - ISSUED BY SP OFFICE

VERIFICATION OF CHARACTER AND ANTECEDENTS

PROCEDURE

  1. Obtain application from nearest eSeva centre
  2. Documents to be enclosed
  3. Residential Proof
  4. ID Proof
  5. Clearly Mention expected salary
  6. Fees payable
  7. In case of individuals - Salary below Rs.3,000/- =Rs.100/- - Salary above Rs.3,000/- = Rs.300/- - Salary above Rs.5,000/- = Rs.500/-
  8. In case of Non Govt Organisations, contract= Rs.1000/=
  9. Contractor character certificate= Rs.1,000/=
  10. Submit to eSeva Centre
  11. Certificate will be issued after 7 days from SP Office

N.O.C.FOR TRANSFER OF VEHICLE TO OTHER STATES

PROCEDURE

  1. Obtain application from nearest eSeva centre
  2. Fill the application
  3. Clearly mention reason for transfer
  4. Type of vehicle
  5. Transfer to which State
  6. Documents to be enclosed
  7. Copy of RC
  8. Fees payable
  9. For two wheelers Rs.100/=
  10. For four wheelers Rs.200/=
  11. Submit to eSeva Centre
  12. Certificate will be issued within 7 days from SP Office

PERMISSIONS

Licence Cyber Cafes/Internet Cafes ▼

LICENCE CYBER CAFES/INTERNET CAFES


PROCEDURE

  1. Obtain application from nearest eSeva centre
  2. Clearly mention name of the company and location with municipal number and Service Provider of internet
  3. Documents to be enclosed:
  4. Latest Municipal Permission
  5. Lease agreement
  6. Site Plan
  7. Rent Receipts
  8. Tax receipts
  9. NOC from Fire Department
  10. Certificate from BSNL if telephone installed
  11. NOC from neighbors
  12. Undertaking regarding not installing gambling machines
  13. ID proof and Residential Proof of organizers
  14. Full particulars of configuration of server and clients installed
  15. Partnership deed
  16. Memorandum of Association
  17. Articles of Association
  18. Indemnity Bond
  19. IT returns, if any
  20. Fees payable:
  21. Rs.60/- for service charges by eSeva
  22. Fresh license fee Rs.2,000/-
  23. Annual Renewal fee Rs.1,000/-
  24. Submit to eSeva Centre
  25. License will be issued after 45 days from SP Office
